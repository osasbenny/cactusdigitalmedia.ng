### Approach 2: "Cactus Brutalism" — Bold Editorial
**Design Movement:** New Wave Brutalism meets Digital Studio

**Core Principles:**
1. High-contrast black and white with strategic green injections
2. Oversized typography as the primary visual element
3. Deliberate use of borders, outlines, and raw grid lines
4. Unexpected layout breaks that signal confidence

**Color Philosophy:**
- Pure black #000000 backgrounds for hero/dark sections
- Pure white #ffffff for content sections
- Cactus green #16a34a as the only accent — used sparingly for maximum impact
- Yellow #fbbf24 as a secondary accent for warnings/highlights

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Hero: massive 120px+ headline with a single green word
- Alternating black/white sections with no gradients
- Portfolio grid with thick black borders
- Services in a numbered list format, not cards

**Signature Elements:**
1. Thick 2px black borders on all interactive elements
2. Oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03) as background watermarks
3. Marquee scrolling text strip between sections

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Hover: instant color inversion (black→white, white→black)
- No rounded corners — sharp edges throughout
- Cursor changes to crosshair on interactive elements

**Animation:**
- Clip-path reveals on scroll
- Text scramble effect on hero
- No easing — linear transitions for brutalist feel

**Typography System:**
- Display: "Bebas Neue" — condensed, powerful
- Body: "DM Sans" — clean, modern

**Design Movement:** Premium Dark SaaS / Craft Studio (Linear + Stripe + Vercel DNA)

1. Deep navy-black backgrounds with luminous white text — feels like a premium product, not a portfolio

3. Emerald/green accent (#10b981) paired with a warm amber highlight (#f59e0b) for dual-tone CTAs

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: oklch(0.08 0.01 250) — deep navy-black
- Surface cards: oklch(0.12 0.015 250) — slightly elevated
- Primary accent: #10b981 (emerald green — cactus brand, growth)
- Secondary accent: #f59e0b (amber — warmth, energy)
- Text primary: #f8fafc, Text secondary: #94a3b8
- Border: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)

- Hero: split layout — left: large headline + CTAs, right: animated device mockup or abstract visual
- Services: 2-column asymmetric grid with numbered cards
- Portfolio: masonry-style grid with hover overlays

- Sticky nav with glass morphism blur effect

1. Three vertical bars (|||) as the brand accent in logo, section dividers, and card decorations

I'm going with **Approach 3: Midnight Craft** — a premium dark studio aesthetic that positions Cactus Digital Media as a top-tier engineering firm.
